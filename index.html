dying_behind_bars

beyond_the_data

this is a digital humanities & data visualization project based on research made public by Reuters at the end of 2020. the research documents deaths in local jails from 2008–2019. this project specifically focuses on female deaths in California jails. 

the purpose of this project is twofold:
1) to understand what this data says and what it doesn't say.
2) to instill in the viewer the necessary reminder that these are Human Beings who deserve to be remembered as more than a statistic or data point.
