beyond_the_data

this is a digital humanities project based on research made public by Reuters at the end of 2020. the research documents deaths in U.S. local jails from 2008â€“2019.
this project specifically focuses on female deaths in California jails.

the purpose of this project is twofold:
1) to critically analyze and understand
what this data says and what it doesn't say.
2) to instill in the viewer the reminder
that these are Human Beings who deserve to be
remembered as more than a statistic or data point.
